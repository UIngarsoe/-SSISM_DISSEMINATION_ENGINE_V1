# Layer 7: Legal & Governance Constraints

**Standalone Academic Module**  
**Title:** Legal and Governance Barriers to Dismantling Scam Networks  
**Date:** 2025-12-15  
**Author:** SSISM Humanitarian Intelligence Cell  

**Key Constraints**  
- Weak or non-existent extradition frameworks in conflict zones  
- Jurisdictional ambiguity in militia-controlled territories  
- Inconsistent application of international sanctions  
- Limited mechanisms for cross-border asset recovery relative to scam profits

**Implication**  
These gaps enable persistence and adaptation of criminal operations despite increased enforcement pressure.
