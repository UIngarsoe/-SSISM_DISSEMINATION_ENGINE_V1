# Layer 4: Actor & Power Map

**Standalone Academic Module**  
**Title:** Actor Analysis and Power Dynamics in Myanmar Scam Ecosystems  
**Date:** 2025-12-15  
**Author:** SSISM Humanitarian Intelligence Cell  

**Primary Actors**  
- Chinese Ministry of Public Security (MPS): Lead external enforcement driver  
- Myanmar military and police authorities: Territorial gatekeepers with limited operational autonomy  
- Border-based militias and armed groups: Operational enablers and protection providers  
- International partners (Interpol, Thai police): Supporting coordination roles  
- Trafficked and coerced victims: Core affected population (multiple nationalities)

**Power Asymmetry**  
Strategic intelligence, financial incentives, and diplomatic leverage originate externally (primarily China). Operational control and territorial access remain fragmented and locally constrained by militia alliances.
