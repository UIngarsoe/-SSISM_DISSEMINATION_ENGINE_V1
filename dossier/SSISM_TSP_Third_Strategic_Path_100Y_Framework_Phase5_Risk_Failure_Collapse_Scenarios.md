## Executive Summary

No democratic transition fails because of ideals.  
Transitions fail because **risks are ignored, denied, or politicised**.

Phase 5 explicitly models **failure pathways**, **collapse triggers**, and **recovery mechanisms** for Myanmar’s Third Strategic Path (TSP).

This phase institutionalises a principle long absent in Myanmar’s history:

> *Planning for failure is an act of responsibility, not pessimism.*

---

## 1. Risk Taxonomy Framework

### 1.1 Risk Categories

All risks are classified into six domains:

1. Political Capture Risks  
2. Security Fragmentation Risks  
3. Economic Shock Risks  
4. Foreign Interference Risks  
5. Institutional Decay Risks  
6. Societal Fracture Risks  

Each risk is evaluated by:
• Probability  
• Impact  
• Velocity  
• Reversibility
